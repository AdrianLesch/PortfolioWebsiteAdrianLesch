Biography

Hi, I’m Adrian, a web developer passionate about crafting digital experiences. Currently, I’m on the Fullstack Engineer Career Path at Codecademy. Welcome to the first iteration of my personal portfolio website, where I’ll share insights about myself, showcase past projects, and provide ways to connect with me.


  • Journey:

    As a child, I was drawn to computers—not just for gaming, but for creating. I vividly remember tinkering with 3D Gamestudio and other easy scripting & creation tools, fueling my curiosity. Later, I pursued a Media-Computer Science degree, merging my love for creativity and technology. Along the way, I explored coding languages, delved into music production, and honed my video and photography editing skills.


  • Coding Adventure Redux:

    After a detour into various sales positions, I felt the pull back to software and web development. In September 2023, I embarked on the Fullstack Engineer Career Path. Prior to that, I completed the Java Course on Codecademy (started in June 2023) to rekindle my coding flame, so I've been actively coding again since seconds!


  • The Present:

    Now, I’m diving headfirst into full-stack web development. My journey has been a blend of passion, persistence, and pixels.


  • Explore My Work:

    My interactive portfolio houses responsive websites, dynamic web apps, and more. Each project tells a unique story. Ready to see my creations? Dive into my projects below!
