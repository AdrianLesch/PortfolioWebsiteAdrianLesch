Biography

Hi, I’m Adrian, a web developer passionate about crafting digital experiences. Currently, I’m on the Fullstack Engineer Career Path at Codecademy. Welcome to the first iteration of my personal portfolio website, where I’ll share insights about myself, showcase past projects, and provide ways to connect with me.

  • Journey:

    As a child, I was drawn to computers—not just for gaming, but for creating. I vividly remember tinkering with 3D Game Maker and other easy scripting & creation tools, fueling my curiosity. Later, I pursued a Media-Computer Science degree, merging my love for creativity and technology. Along the way, I explored coding languages, delved into music production, and honed my video and photography editing skills.
